Optimize Your Adventure Fuel

Every great adventure begins with a well-crafted food plan. Whether you're scaling mountains, paddling rivers, or cycling through valleys, your success depends on keeping you and your team energized at all times.

Provisioner helps you meticulously plan your food for hiking, kayaking, sailing, bike touring or any other adventure requiring provisions. Take full control of your pack weight and nutritional needs to ensure you have the energy to explore further.

Detailed Food Planning

Define adventure details like duration, physiology of your crew, activity levels and use your own custom recipes.

Weight Optimization

Provisioner calculates the required weight of every meal and ingredient, minimizing costs and your load while adventuring.

Nutrition Control

Manage macro-nutrient ratios (protein, fat, carbs) for meals and your entire trip to match your needs.
